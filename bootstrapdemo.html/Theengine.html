The engine

VQ35DE (2003-2005)

The VQ35DE is the first engine used in the 350z, it is a reliable engine mainly constructed out of a aluminium body, it pushes 280BHP on stock and it could rev to 6.2K.

VQ35DE Revup(2006)

This model is a upgraded version over the older DE engine it had a higher Rev range of 6.4k and 300HP 30 more than the older DE engine.

VQ35HR (2007-2009)

this is the engine that had the real power, pushing at 306HP and a higher Rev range of 7k this had alot more over the previous engines like a new twin intake over the previous
single intake, better oil seals, the flow of the HR engine and better technology made the car a great high 13 second car beating the older DE engines, its not to say that the DE engines are bad.
